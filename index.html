About Me

I am Mike Makmur, a second year graduate student at the University of Minnesota's Institute for Astrophysics. Currently, I am working on my Master's thesis with Patrick Kelly, studying Type IIn supernovae. I graduated with a Bachelor's of Science in Astrophysics from Rutgers University in 2018.

In my spare time, I enjoy playing a variety of sports including football and soccer. I am a huge fan of the New England Patriots and all things Rutgers Scarlet Knights. I am also an instructor for Piscataway Martial Arts, a children's martial arts class based in my hometown of Piscataway, New Jersey.

Teaching

I am a teaching assistant for one section of AST 1001: Exploring the Universe. I teach Sections 109 (T 10:10-12:05), 116 (W 10:10-12:05), and 124 (Th 1:25-3:20).
